Welcome to funwithaki.com.
This website documents the work made by Kevin Cadena in Aki Nurosi’s class during the Fall semester of 2014 at the Rhode Island School of Design.

Oh? What's this to left? It was our first project in the class. We were given 8 emotive and 8 expressive colors with an assigned sense to think about the color in [Ex. A Loud color in terms of Sound]. Then match those color swatches we painted with objects from real life but the catch was, because of photography and printing, the color might've come out different! So we had to compensate for that and through it, we began to think about color in a more refined way.

There were five major assignments in the class. To view those assignments, simply click one of the icons at the top to learn more about specific project.

Thank you for viewing!
Kevin Cadena GD ‘16
